## Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub-and-Spoke Model
- Each pillar article links to all supporting articles in its category
- Supporting articles link back to pillar and to 1-2 related articles
- All blog posts include contextual links to relevant tool pages

### Best Practices
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to tool pages from problem-solution content
- Create "Related Articles" sections at end of posts
- Update older posts with links to newer relevant content
